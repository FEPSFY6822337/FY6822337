Abstract

        This paper investigates how facial recognition technology works, and the issues that would be faced with its implementation in
        society. Although facial recognition technology is not yet accurate enough to be used for certain situations, it should eventually
        reach a level where it can be used effectively. However, many issues may arise with its implementation, as data privacy and security
        concerns increase, which could lead to a more technologically dystopian society.
